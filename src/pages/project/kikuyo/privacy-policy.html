Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2025

chiiQoon ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Kikuyo mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Important: This App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

This App does not collect any personal information.

However, the following anonymized information may be automatically collected through Google Play Store:

  • Crash reports (when the app terminates unexpectedly)
  • Basic usage statistics (install count, uninstall count, etc.)
  • Basic device information (Android version, device model)

This information is collected through Google Play Console and is used solely for improving app quality.

2. How We Use Information

The anonymous data collected through Google Play is used only for:

  • Improving app stability and fixing bugs
  • Checking device compatibility
  • Enhancing app quality

3. Data Storage

All data created within the App (search history, favorites, etc.) is stored only on your device. This data is never transmitted to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

This App does not use any third-party analytics tools or tracking services.

However, basic usage statistics may be collected by the Google Play Store system. For details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no risk of collecting children's personal information.

6. Data Deletion

To delete data created by this App:

  • Uninstalling the app will delete all local data
  • You can also clear app data from Android settings

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
