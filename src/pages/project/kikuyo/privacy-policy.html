Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2026

chiiQoon ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Kikuyo mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Summary: This App collects minimal data necessary for in-app purchases and app improvement. We do not collect personally identifiable information.

1. Information We Collect

This App does not collect any personally identifiable information.

However, the following information may be collected:

Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases, the following information is processed through RevenueCat (our payment processing service):

  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Anonymous user identifiers
  • Transaction timestamps

This information is used solely for managing your purchases and subscriptions.

Diagnostic Data

The following anonymized information may be automatically collected through Google Play Store:

  • Crash reports (when the app terminates unexpectedly)
  • Basic usage statistics (install count, uninstall count, etc.)
  • Basic device information (Android version, device model)

This information is collected through Google Play Console and is used solely for improving app quality.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used only for:

  • Managing and verifying your in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Improving app stability and fixing bugs
  • Checking device compatibility
  • Enhancing app quality

3. Data Storage

All content data created within the App (search history, favorites, etc.) is stored only on your device. This data is never transmitted to external servers.

Purchase-related data is securely stored by RevenueCat to manage your subscription status.

4. Third-Party Services

This App uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat - For managing in-app purchases and subscriptions. Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Store - For app distribution and basic usage statistics. Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

This App is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

6. Data Deletion

To delete data created by this App:

  • Uninstalling the app will delete all local data
  • You can also clear app data from Android settings
  • For purchase history managed by RevenueCat, please contact us at the email below

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
